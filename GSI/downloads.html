Other Files

Permissiver & Zygote Fix are not needed on any GSI which is built after 25th September. If you are flashing any GSI which was built before this date then you have to use permissiver & zygote fix (For Android 10 A-only devices). A/B devices never needs zygote fix.

1. Permissiver - is the zip which is used to force SELinux to be permissive, because some vendors enforce SELinux which stops GSIs from booting.

2. Zygote Fix - This zip is needed to be flashed if you are flashing an Android 10-based GSIs on A-only devices. A/B users don't need it.

2. Google Play Services APK - Whenever you boot into a GSI the first thing you should do is to install the Google Play Services APK. Installing it will clear the "System Updating..." notification and you will be able to login to your Google account .

For Android 9 - Choose the arm64-v8a + armeabi-v7a - nodpi - Android 9.0+
For Android 10 - Choose the arm64-v8a + armeabi-v7a - nodpi - Android 10+
