ErfanGSIs

These are the basic requirements for flashing a GSI on your phone. If you don't understand the words "Unlocked Bootloader" then please use Google search and get yourself familiar with these things.

Basic Requirements

  1. An Android device with Treble support
    (Oreo 8.1 or Pie)
  2. Unlocked Bootloader and TWRP installed

A-only or A/B?

There are two type of devices with Treble support, A-only and A/B. If you want to check if your device is A-only or A/B then download Treble Support Check. If the app indicates that Seamless System Updates are unsupported then your device is A-only and if it says supported then your devices is A/B. However, if your device launched with Android Pie out of the box, then you should always flash A/B GSIs.

Which vendor sir?

Okay, this is the most asked question sometimes. Which vendor should I use? The answer is - try all vendors. There are different ROMs out there with Treble support. If a GSI does not boot with one vendor then try another. Asking for vendor links in the main ErfanGSIs group is not a good idea because there are few chances of getting it answered. You should ask for vendor/ROM links to your device community.

People who have MTK and Exynos chipset based devices have fewer chances of booting ErfanGSIs. Some people out there with devices which are not arm64 don't have much for them because most of the GSIs needs arm64 arch support (64-bit), but there is a MIUI GSI with arm support which is listed on ErfanGSIs downloads site.
